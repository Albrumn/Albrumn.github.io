Having trouble navigating the many channels of the Duolingo Discord server? Here's a tool that can help.

It adds visual styling to the navigation sidebar to assist in quickly finding the channel you're looking for. Sections are emphasized with a thin border and drop-shadow.

Language categories are marked with that language's flag, matching Duolingo's style. Non-language categories are marked with other Duo-related icons.

Note: This will effect the navigation sidebar styling of other Discord servers as well.
